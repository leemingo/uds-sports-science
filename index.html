About Us

KAISports brings together researchers, engineers, and practitioners who believe AI can change how sports are studied and played. We share creative ideas across academia and industry to push the boundaries of sports analytics.

Sports Analysis

Develop data-driven methods to understand match dynamics, tactics, and individual contributions.

Game Strategy

Quantify decisions and uncover insights that help coaches and analysts plan and adapt.

Athlete Performance

Combine tracking, GPS, and physiological signals to understand and improve athlete performance.

What We Do

We publish at top venues such as KDD, CIKM, ECML PKDD, MIT Sloan Sports Analytics Conference, and Hudl Performance Insights, and collaborate with leagues and clubs around the world.
