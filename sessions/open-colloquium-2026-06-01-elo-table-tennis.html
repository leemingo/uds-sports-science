Using ELO for Grading in Table Tennis Courses

A Sports Analytics presentation on adapting ELO-style rating ideas for educational assessment in table tennis teaching contexts.

Presenter
Abdelrahman Abdels
Chair / Department
Sports Analytics
Format

Overview

Session Focus

This session looks at whether competitive rating systems such as ELO can be translated into meaningful grading logic for table tennis courses. It is positioned as a concise methods-focused talk suited for discussion across teaching, analytics, and applied sports evaluation.
