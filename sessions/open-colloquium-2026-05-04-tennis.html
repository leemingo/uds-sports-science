Game, Set, Match—A Finite Stochastic Markov Chain Approach for In-Match Win Predictions in Professional Tennis

Master's thesis final presentation on probabilistic win prediction within professional tennis using a finite stochastic Markov chain framework.

Overview

Session Focus

This talk presents a tennis-focused probabilistic modeling approach for estimating in-match win likelihood. As a master's thesis final, it fits the colloquium's goal of surfacing concrete research outputs in a concise format that invites cross-chair feedback.
